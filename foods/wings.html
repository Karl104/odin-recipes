Garlic Butter Chicken Wings



If you love chicken wings like us but are looking for a variation from the classics like buffalo wings, then you must try this rich and comforting recipe. Although they're full of flavor, they're also milder than a lot of chicken wing recipes if you're looking to avoid anything too spicy.


Heres the Ingredients:

  • chicken wings
  • kosher salt
  • black pepper
  • Butter with Canola Oil
  • dried granulated garlic
  • dried chives
  • hot sauce
  • Ranch or blue cheese sauce for dipping

Heres the Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400ËšF. Line two baking sheets with foil and place wire baking racks on top. Lay out wings on wire racks and season with salt and pepper.

  2. Bake chicken wings for 30 minutes.

  3. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan stir together butter with canola oil, granulated garlic, chives, and hot sauce over low heat. Keep warm but dont simmer the mixture.

  4. Place wings back on baking racks and spoon extra garlic butter mixture on each wing.

  5. Bake for a second time for 10-15 minutes until wings are crispy.

  6. Serve baked wings with ranch or blue cheese sauce for dipping!
